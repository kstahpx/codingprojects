Music

The only people who don't like music are serial killers.

Music is the key to life. It can help individuals through really hard times: when you're doing homework and all motivation just seeps from your body, when it's taking extra long to wash the dishes, and just many of the simple downsides to everyday life. There are multiple music streaming services that are dedicated to getting you your music quickly and beautifully, for example, Youtube, Spotify, and iTunes.

Everyone has different music tastes. For me personally, I'm open to listening to all types of music EXCEPT FOR COUNTRY. I listen to hip-hop, rnb, rock, alternative rock, and more.
